About SATL
SATL Freight Nigeria Limited is a subsidiary of SATL Freight Pty, South Africa, founded in 2013. SATL Freight Nigeria Limited is primarily a container logistics provider that services the shipping industry and offers a one stop solution to all clients.
Directors
Ekemini Amos
Director
Mr Ekemini Thompson Amos is a founding Director of SATL Nigeria. Mr. Amos holds a Bachelor of Engineering Degree (B.Eng) in Mechanical Engineering, from the University of Bolton, UK. He is a member of the Royal Institution of Mechanical Engineers, a member of American Society of Mechanical Engineers, and an Alumnus of London Business School.
